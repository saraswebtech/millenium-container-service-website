    CONTAINER REPAIR SERVICES

    Providing Professional Container Repair Solutions

    With skilled technicians and well-planned repair processes, MCS delivers reliable container repair solutions to keep your equipment operational and compliant.

    Millennium Container Services (MCS) operates a dedicated container repair depot equipped with modern repair infrastructure and well-planned work areas to support efficient container maintenance.


    Our facility is capable of handling general container repairs, ranging from minor dents, scrapes, and surface damage to major structural repairs, including side wall and roof panel replacement. Repairs and inserts can be carried out on sills, corner posts, rails, or complete components can be replaced as required.


    With controlled repair processes and skilled manpower, MCS ensures containers are restored to safe, operational, and industry-acceptable standards, supporting uninterrupted logistics operations.
