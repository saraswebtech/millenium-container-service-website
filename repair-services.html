Our Repair Services

CONTAINER REPAIR SERVICES

Millennium container services (MCS) operates a fully equipped container repair and refurbishment depot, featuring a purpose-built semi-automatic shot blasting booth along with complete painting and descaling facilities. Our infrastructure is designed to handle containers efficiently while maintaining high quality and safety standards. The depot undertakes a full range of container repair works, from minor surface damage such as dents and abrasions to major structural repairs including side wall and roof panel replacement. Repairs and insert work can be carried out on sills, corner posts, rails, or complete components, depending on the condition of the container.

Advantages & Capabilities

  • Reduced repair turnaround time through optimized workflows .
  • Dedicated paint shop with capacity for up to 2 TEU containers
  • Environment-friendly container washing system with a closed water-cycle process
  • Excellent connectivity with direct motorway access for smooth container movement

Maintenance

MCS has an in-house maintenance team across all operational locations, responsible for planned and preventive maintenance of equipment to ensure uninterrupted operations and consistent service quality.
